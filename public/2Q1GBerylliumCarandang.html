STUDYING TIPS FOR PISAY STUDENTS

Many newcoming PSHS students often encounter stories from their fellow schoolmates from upper batches, who say that time-management is a very important thing in the life of a Pisay student. Yet, being time-productive is easier said than done, and many students at first struggle with accomplishing this. Thus, the goal of this webpage is to help students who still struggle to keep their attention and focus in order to be productive, by providing some PRACTICAL tips that I personally find useful myself.
